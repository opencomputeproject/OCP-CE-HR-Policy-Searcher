## Executive Summary

Grid Cell 1.5 focuses on UK government programs offering grants, loans, tax incentives, and investment support relevant to data center heat reuse projects. Key findings include:

1. **Industrial Energy Transformation Fund (IETF)** - Data centers explicitly eligible; waste heat recovery for district heating export allowed. PROGRAM CLOSED but critical policy precedent.

2. **National Wealth Fund** - £27.8bn capitalization; £4bn local authority lending; streamlined GHNF access. Key financing vehicle for heat network infrastructure.

3. **Investment Zones & Freeports** - Tax incentives (100% business rates relief, enhanced capital allowances) available in designated areas. Extended to 2031-2034.

4. **Devolved Programs** - Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland have separate energy efficiency grant programs with varying eligibility and focus.

5. **Enhanced Capital Allowances ENDED** - The ECA scheme for energy-efficient equipment closed April 2020. Full expensing (100% first-year) now broadly available.

### Investment Zones & Freeports

**Investment Zones (12 UK total):**
- £80m per zone over 5 years (£160m over 10 years)
- Tax sites: max 600 hectares
- 100% business rates relief
- Enhanced capital allowances
- Employer NI relief (£2,200/employee/year savings)
- University-anchored knowledge clusters
- Focus: digital/tech, green industries

**Freeports (8 England + Scottish/Welsh Green Freeports):**
- Same tax incentives as Investment Zones
- PLUS customs/VAT benefits
- Extended to Sept 2031 (England), 2034 (Scotland/Wales)
- £25m per Freeport for land/infrastructure
- Grid connections identified as key barrier (4+ year waits)

## Data Center Heat Reuse Relevance

### Directly Applicable Programs

1. **Green Heat Network Fund (GHNF)** - Already covered in Grid Cell 1.3, but GHNF applicants get streamlined NWF/UKIB access

2. **National Wealth Fund Local Authority Lending** - £4bn at gilts+40bps for LA infrastructure including heat networks

3. **IETF/SIETF** (if reopened) - Explicit data center eligibility; waste heat to district heating allowed

4. **Investment Zones** - Data centers in knowledge clusters could access tax incentives

### Supporting Programs

1. **Scottish SME Loan** - Interest-free loans for heating/cooling equipment upgrades
2. **NI Energy Efficiency Capital Grant** - Up to £150K for energy efficiency (when reopened)
3. **Welsh Future Proofing Fund** - £5-10K for energy efficiency improvements

### Policy Gaps Identified

1. **No direct data center heat reuse mandate** (unlike some EU jurisdictions)
2. **IETF closure** - Major loss of industrial decarbonisation funding without successor
3. **ECA scheme ended** - No dedicated tax incentive for energy-efficient equipment (though full expensing provides similar benefit)
4. **Fragmented devolved programs** - Different eligibility, thresholds, and focus across UK nations
